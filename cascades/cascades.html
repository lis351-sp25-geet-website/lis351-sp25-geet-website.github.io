North Cascades, WA

Easily one of the most underrated national parks out there. Easilly day-tripplable from Seattle, very drivable and accessible, and you get great views of the Cascades and some other nice georaphical features.

Note that while I include Washington Pass in the photo, it is not in the national park boundaries, but it is very closeby.
