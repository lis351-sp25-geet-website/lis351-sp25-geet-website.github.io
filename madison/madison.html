Madison, WI

Well, if it isn't the place I live.

Surprisingly, Madison has a lot of great places and landmarks. However, there is really only one that stands out.

The Wisconsin State Capitol was made in 1917 and houses all 3 branches of the Wisconsin state government.

It is arguably one of the best state capitols I have seen.
Wisconsin has enacted a height restriction preventing Madison buildings from blocking the Capitol view, so I can also get good pictures of the Madison Skyline, shown below.
